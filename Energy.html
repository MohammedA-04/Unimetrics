The SI unit of time is the second. From here the minute (60 seconds) and the hour (3600 seconds) are derived. A day is assumed to be 86,400 seconds for the purposes of this calculator although this can vary with the insertion of a "leap second".

Seconds to Hours Formula

1. (x)s / 3600s = x.y Hrs | note: there are 3600 seconds in an hour
2. x.yy * 60m = x.yy Hrs | To convert the decimal to minutes, multiply it by 60
2.1 So, you only need to convert 0.5556 to an hour: 0.5556 × 60 = 33.336. So, 5,600 seconds is equal to about 1 hour, 33 minutes.

Hours to Seconds Formula

1. Hours * 3600s = Seconds

Seconds to Days Formula

1. Seconds / 86400s = Days | note: there are 86400 seconds in a day

Days to Seconds Formula

1. Days * 86400s = Days in Seconds

Minutes to Days Formula

1. Minutes / 1440 = Days

Days to Minutes Formula

1. Days * 1440 = Minutes
